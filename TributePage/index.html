Mahendra Bahubali Dhoni

Career Summary

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, fondly known as "Captain Cool," is widely regarded as one of the most successful and inspiring captains in the history of Indian cricket. Hailing from Ranchi, Jharkhand, Dhoni made his international debut in 2004, quickly establishing himself as a powerhouse with his aggressive batting style and exceptional wicket-keeping skills.

Under Dhoni's captaincy, India reached the pinnacle of Test cricket rankings in 2009 and achieved numerous historic victories. His finishing abilities in limited-overs cricket are legendary, with his iconic last-ball six in the 2011 World Cup final cementing his legacy. After an illustrious career, he retired from international cricket in 2020 but continues to play in the IPL, where he captains the Chennai Super Kings.
